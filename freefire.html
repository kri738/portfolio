About Free Fire

One of the world's most popular Battle Royale games.

Free Fire is a free-to-play battle royale game developed and published by Garena. Released in 2017, it quickly became one of the most downloaded mobile games worldwide.


The game is famous for its fast-paced 10-minute matches, unique playable characters, exciting battle royale maps, pets, vehicles, and one of the biggest mobile esports scenes.
