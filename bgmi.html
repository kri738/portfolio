About BGMI

Battlegrounds Mobile India (BGMI) is India's official version of PUBG Mobile, developed by Krafton. It offers realistic battle royale gameplay, exciting maps, competitive esports, and regular updates for millions of players across India.

BGMI delivers an immersive survival experience where 100 players parachute onto a battlefield, collect weapons, drive vehicles, and eliminate opponents while surviving inside the shrinking safe zone. Players can enjoy Solo, Duo, and Squad modes, participate in ranked matches, complete Royale Pass missions, and compete in India's biggest esports tournaments.
