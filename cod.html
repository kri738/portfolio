About Call of Duty Mobile

Call of Duty: Mobile brings the legendary FPS franchise to mobile devices with console-quality graphics, exciting multiplayer modes, Battle Royale, iconic maps and operators.

Developed by TiMi Studio Group and published by Activision, Call of Duty Mobile offers fast-paced combat, realistic weapons, ranked multiplayer, seasonal Battle Pass rewards, zombies events and one of the biggest competitive mobile gaming communities.
